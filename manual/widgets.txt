bbWidgets contains a tree structure of widgets
widgets are drawn to the screen top to bottom, left to right

layout (root)
   - viewport, dialog, prompt, command
   - spellmenu
       - spellselects
   - spellbar
       - spellslots
   - connect (to server)
       - ipaddress (textbox)
       - port (textbox)
       - connect button
   - top left bar
       - currentspell
       - connecticon
   - toy widgets (shpere, card, box, button)

spellbar and connect should not be visible at the same time
