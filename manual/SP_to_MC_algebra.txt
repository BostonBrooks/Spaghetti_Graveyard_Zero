    SP.x = VP->width/2.0
            + (MC.i - VP->viewpoint.i)
            + (MC.j - VP->viewpoint.j);

    SP.y = VP->height/2.0
            + (MC.i - VP->viewpoint.i)
            - (MC.j - VP->viewpoint.j)
            - (MC.k - VP->viewpoint.k);

Let:

SP.x = X
SP.y = Y
VP->width/2.0 = W
VP->height/2.0 = H
MC.i = I
MC.j = J
MC.k = 0

VP->viewpoint.i = L
VP->viewpoint.j = M
VP->viewpoint.k = N

Then:

X = W + I – L + J – M
Y = H + I – L – J + M – 0 + N

Solve for I and J

I + J = X – W + L + N
I – J = Y – H + L – M – N

2I = (I + J) + (I – J)
2I = (X – W + L + N) + (Y – H + L – M – N)
2I = X + Y – W – H + 2L – M
I = (X + Y – W – H + 2L – M)/2

2J = (I + J) – (I – J)
2J = (X – W + L + N) - (Y – H + L – M – N)
2J = X – Y – W + H + M + 2N
J = (X – Y – W + H + M + 2N)/2

And so:

MC.i = (SP.x + SP.y – VP->width/2.0 – VP->height/2.0 + 2*VP->viewpoint.i – VP->viewpoint.j)/2

MC.j = (SP.x – SP.y – VP->width/2.0 + VP->height/2.0 + VP->viewpoint.j + 2*VP→viewpoint.k)/2

MC.j = 0;
